Candle Holder

The Candle Holder was inspired by Frank Lloyd Wright's iconic designs. It was created in AutoCAD, then configured with laser cutting and glued together to keep the pieces in place. A stain with hues of purple and pink was meant to be a spin-off of the designer's most-used colors in his houses. Using a similar color also elevated certain elements that draw attention to the side designs, such as circles used to create a sense of fluid motion.
