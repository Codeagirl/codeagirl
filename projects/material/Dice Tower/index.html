Dice Tower

Backstory

During my Dungeons & Dragons game dice become an essential part, deciding the course of the game. During a game sometime the dice can see like they are fighting against what you are trying to do, this is commenly refered to as cursed dice. This dice tower was created in an act to elimate the amount of cursed dice roles, to provide a better style of rolling dice, that would limmit humman error. By making the interaction between the human and dice less when a dice roll bad you can yell at the dice without any need to feel like the role was caused by an error you made.

Creation process

This dice tower was made using Autocad. It was modeled by hand, each while also utilizing some public designs for the door and windows. The interior of the tower and outside case are two different part and were printed separately. During this design and bulid process I encountered the error of 3d printers, meaning that they do not print exactly they print with small error changing the size of both peaces. This ment that my first print of the pieces did not it into each other. I overcame this problem by making the inside piece a little smaller and makeing the outside piece a little bigger.
